This is the seed post for the new `/blog` archive. If you can read this with the right typography, the scaffolding is working.

## What lives here

Field notes on the things I'm building. Short retrospectives, design rationales, the occasional rant about tooling.

The plan is to keep posts small and frequent rather than long and rare.

## A code sample, for fidelity

```ts
const blog = defineCollection({
  loader: glob({ pattern: '**/*.{md,mdx}', base: './src/content/blog' }),
  schema: ({ image }) =>
    z.object({
      title: z.string(),
      pubDate: z.coerce.date(),
      tags: z.array(z.string()).default([]),
    }),
})
```

## A list, for variety

1. Numbered entries grouped by year
